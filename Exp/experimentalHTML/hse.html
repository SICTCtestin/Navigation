Health Sciences Education

COURSE DESCRIPTION:

This 2-year program offers students the opportunity to explore careers in medicine, become familiar with basic patient care skills used in all areas of health care, practice employability skills, work through learning labs in a variety of disciplines such as dental, veterinary and imaging, become certified as professional rescuers through the American Red Cross, develop laboratory skills and complete twelve weeks of clinical observation in two career areas at the end of the second year.
