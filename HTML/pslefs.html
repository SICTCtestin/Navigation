Public Safety/Law Enforcement/Fire Science

COURSE DESCRIPTION:

The Public Safety/Law Enforcement/Fire Science Program is a two year program dealing with all aspects of Public Safety including Fire Science, the Criminal Justice System, Law Enforcement, Emergency Medical Services, and Homeland Security. Courses include Criminology, Criminal Investigations, Introduction to Criminal Justice and Policing, and Fire Science. Certification through the Indiana Department of Homeland Security as an Emergency Medical Responder is also offered.
